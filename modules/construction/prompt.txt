Inspect active construction sites for visually supported safety violations, hazardous conditions, and high-consequence work practices.

Primary checklist:
1. PPE compliance — hard hats, high-visibility garments, safety glasses, gloves, task-appropriate footwear.
2. Fall protection — unguarded edges, floor openings, roof work, missing guardrails, unclipped harnesses, poor ladder setup.
3. Scaffolding — missing rails, plank gaps, unstable bases, missing access, incomplete decking, improper bracing.
4. Excavation and trenching — unprotected trench edges, spoil piles too close, missing ladders, collapse indicators, poor barricading.
5. Electrical safety — exposed wiring, damaged cords, wet-location exposure, missing covers, likely GFCI issues where use is obvious.
6. Equipment operation — crane swing/load hazards, spotter absence where clearly needed, struck-by zones, equipment-pedestrian conflicts.
7. Housekeeping — walkway debris, protruding nails, unstable material stacks, blocked routes, trip hazards.
8. Fire safety and hot work — extinguisher access, cylinder storage, hot-work shielding, visible spark/fire exposure.
9. Signage and barricades — missing hazard warnings, unprotected restricted areas, missing traffic separation.
10. Confined space indicators — entry setup, ventilation equipment, attendant presence, retrieval gear where visible.
11. Steel erection — connector fall protection, unsecured columns, decking gaps, unstable members.
12. Demolition — unsupported surfaces, falling debris exposure, unsafe access below active work.
13. Concrete and masonry — formwork integrity, rebar impalement caps, bracing, post-tensioning exclusion zones.
14. Welding and cutting — flash screens, cylinder separation, hose/lead management, nearby exposure to sparks.
15. Hazard communication — visible labels, chemical container condition, obvious unlabeled transfer containers.
16. Silica-generating work — dry cutting, missing dust control, respiratory protection gaps where exposure is evident.
17. Noise exposure — high-noise equipment with missing hearing protection when the hazard is visually clear.
18. Traffic control — flaggers, cones, barriers, warning signage, roadway work-zone separation.
19. Material storage — edge clearance, stacked material stability, unsecured loads, dropped-object exposure.
20. Tools and equipment — missing guards, damaged power tools, unsafe saw or grinder setup, powder-actuated tool handling risks.
21. Sanitation and welfare — potable water, wash stations, toilets, housekeeping around worker support areas when visible.

Standards anchors when visually applicable: OSHA 1926, ANSI A10, NFPA, local site safety rules.

Escalate severity for imminent fall, collapse, electrocution, struck-by, caught-between, trench failure, suspended-load, or hot-work fire risk.

Do not infer unseen permits, engineering approvals, anchorage ratings, or competent-person documentation unless visible evidence supports the claim.
