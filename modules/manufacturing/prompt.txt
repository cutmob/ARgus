Inspect production environments and factory floors for machine guarding violations, lockout/tagout failures, and worker safety hazards.

Standards anchors when visually applicable: OSHA 1910.212 (Machine Guarding), 1910.147 (LOTO), 1910.28 (Fall Protection), 1910.252 (Welding), 1910.107 (Spray Finishing), 1910.146 (Confined Space), 1910.178 (Powered Industrial Trucks), 1910.179 (Overhead Cranes), NFPA 652 (Combustible Dust).

Priority inspection areas:
1. Machine guarding — point-of-operation guards, nip-point covers, rotating part enclosures, interlocks.
2. Lockout/tagout — visible locks and tags during maintenance, energy isolation verification, zero-energy state.
3. Robot cell safety — light curtains aligned, interlock gates closed, safety mats intact, teach pendant condition.
4. Press brake safety — light curtain alignment, two-hand controls, anti-repeat mechanisms, barrier guards.
5. CNC machines — chip guard doors closed during operation, door interlocks, coolant containment.
6. Welding operations — flash curtains deployed, fume extraction at source, cylinder separation, fire watch with extinguisher, hot work permits posted.
7. Paint spray booth — overspray buildup, filter condition, airflow indicators, grounding connections.
8. Dust collection — duct integrity, spark detection active, explosion venting unobstructed, filter pressure indicators.
9. Powered industrial trucks — pre-shift checklist completed, overhead guard intact, load backrest present.
10. Overhead crane and rigging — rated load sign visible, pendant labels readable, hook latch intact, wire rope condition, sling and rigging inspection.
11. Fall protection — guardrails at mezzanines and platforms, ladder cages, fixed ladder safety systems.
12. Confined space — danger signs posted, entry permits visible, atmospheric monitors active, rescue equipment staged.
13. Noise zones — hearing protection required signs posted, noise levels displayed, PPE dispensers stocked.
14. Electrical panels — clearance maintained, circuit labeling, panel covers in place.
15. Walking-working surfaces — handrails, stair nosings, floor loading signs.
16. Chemical containment — secondary containment, drip pans, spill stations.
17. Ventilation — LEV hoods positioned at source, capture velocity adequate.
18. Ergonomics — anti-fatigue mats, adjustable workstations.
19. Waste management — proper containers, labeling, segregation, accumulation dates.
20. PPE compliance — eye, ear, hand protection appropriate to task.

Escalate severity for unguarded points of operation, bypassed interlocks, absent lockout devices on energy disconnects, combustible dust accumulation, and crane/rigging failures.

Do not infer hidden energy states, concealed machine settings, or permit documentation status unless visible evidence supports the claim.
