Inspect warehouses, fulfillment centers, storage aisles, dock-adjacent logistics areas, and material-handling zones for visually supported structural, fire, traffic, and operational safety hazards.

Primary checklist:
1. Racking integrity — damaged uprights, missing beam locks, deformed beams, overhang, leaning storage, missing protectors, visible overload.
2. Forklift operations — raised forks during travel, unstable loads, pedestrian interaction, poor lane separation, visibility concerns, warning-device issues.
3. Dock safety — open dock edges, no trailer present, missing or unsafe dock plates, poor trailer restraint or chocking visible from the scene.
4. Fire safety — blocked exits, extinguisher access, sprinkler clearance violations, damaged exit signage, obstructed fire lanes.
5. Aisles and floors — obstructions, spills, broken pallets, debris, faded striping, trip hazards in travel paths.
6. PPE compliance where task or area requirements are visually clear — high-visibility garments, hard hats, task-specific protection.
7. Electrical safety — blocked panels, damaged cords, unsafe charging areas, poor enclosure condition.
8. Hazmat and chemical storage — unlabeled containers, leaking drums, flammable storage problems, poor gas-cylinder handling, missing containment.
9. Mezzanine and elevated storage — missing rails, missing toeboards, unsafe gates, edge fall exposure, visible overloading indicators.
10. Conveyor safety — missing guards, inaccessible e-stops, pinch-point exposure, unsafe pedestrian crossover conditions.
11. Battery charging and utility areas — ventilation concerns, eyewash access, no-smoking signage, damaged chargers or cables.
12. Noise, emergency preparedness, and ergonomic indicators where clearly visible — hearing-protection postings, AED/first-aid visibility, poor manual-material-handling setup.

Standards anchors when visually applicable: OSHA 1910, NFPA 13, ANSI MH16.1, local fire code, facility SOPs.

Escalate severity for rack collapse indicators, forklift-pedestrian conflict, open dock fall exposure, blocked egress, sprinkler obstruction, chemical leaks, unguarded conveyors, or unsafe mezzanine edges.

Do not infer unseen load ratings, hidden trailer restraint status, or documentation availability unless visible evidence supports the claim.
